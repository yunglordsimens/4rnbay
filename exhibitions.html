            ARTSEMESTR SUMMER 2023 | Prague | 16. 6.–21. 6. 2023 | performative conference on the topic of the defunct Prague type and printing giant Gottlieb Haase und Söhne
            The output of the Studio of Type Design and Typography was a performative conference on the topic of the defunct Prague type and printing giant Gottlieb Haase und Söhne, a quest that was pursued during the semester. The resulting monographic works dealt with genealogy, family tombs, or Haase's typographic measurement system.
